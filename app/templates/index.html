Chatter Mapper

Mapping Twitter Activity Across San Francisco

  • Where is everybody? In a big city, it can be difficult find this out. Websearches may describe 'shopping districts' or parks, but its often hard to determine where popular parks, street festivals, or other various congregations of people are located withoout painstakingly visiting every block by foot or car.

    Twitter is a fantistic reservoir of information about where people are, and what they have to say. To uncover local hotspots, I mapped the frequency of tweets and message content across the neighborhoods of San Francisco.

tweet clouds

See the average twitter activity for each neighborhood of San Francisco over two weeks. Word clouds highlight the most common words for each block.

tweet rythms

The cyclical pattern of tweeting over the day and week for every block can be grouped in to six clusters. See which blocks have similar activity patterns.

tweets by day

I broke activity down into weekends, weekdays, and different times of day. See the maps for just weekend or weekday activity here!
